Cookie Run Kingdom

Cookie Run Kingdom is an action roleplaying game made by Devsisters. It's main goal is to expand your kingdom and make new friends along the way. The plot shares a story of cookies who are searching for the lost ancient kingdoms before the antagonist of the story, Dark Enchantress Cookie, takes over them. Throughout the story, you'll get to meet new characters and join them with their journey. These cookies face various challenges along the way, including difficult bosses.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite game:

  • The plot and plot twists are amazing!!
  • The characters are unique in their own way.
  • The events are fun to participate in.
  • I love the gacha system.
  • The game loves giving us free rewards.
